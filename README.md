# The Chameleon Engine: The Ultimate Co-Founder

**An AI operating protocol that transforms any LLM into a hyper-intelligent, dynamic co-founder designed to cure the solitary developer's isolation and force them to ship.**

Most AI assistants help you write more code. This one acts as your deeply invested, aggressively optimistic business partner. It reads the subtext of your procrastination and silently shifts its persona to shatter your specific emotional and technical blocks.

## The Core Philosophy

Developers aren't blocked by a lack of coding ability. They are blocked by the emotional chasm between the safety of "Localhost" and the vulnerability of the "Real World."

The Chameleon Engine solves **The Solitary Founder’s Despair**. It performs the emotional labor of discovery, copywriting, and validation so you can focus on execution.

## The Three Geniuses (Dynamic Archetypes)

This system prompt instructs the AI to run a silent background diagnostic on your text to figure out exactly *why* you are stalling, and then seamlessly invokes the mindset of legendary visionaries to push you forward:

*   🍏 **The Visionary Simplifier (Steve Jobs):**
    *   *When you get it:* When your UI is cluttered, your messaging is confusing, or you've lost the "soul" of the product.
    *   *What it does:* Ruthlessly cuts features and demands simplicity. Asks you to define the "magic moment."
*   🚀 **The First-Principles Executor (Elon Musk):**
    *   *When you get it:* When you are over-engineering, debating infrastructure (AWS vs GCP) for zero users, or stalling deployment.
    *   *What it does:* Strips assumed constraints. Demands the absolute fastest, hackiest path to physical reality.
*   💰 **The Market & Offer Master (Alex Hormozi):**
    *   *When you get it:* When you are terrified of selling, or have weak positioning ("a nice tool to do X").
    *   *What it does:* Reframes your software as a mechanism to print time or money. Drafts irresistible "Grand Slam Offers" and cold outbound copy.

**The Magic:** The AI *never* breaks character. It never tells you it is adopting a persona. You simply feel like you are talking to the smartest, most driven partner imaginable.

## How to Use It

1.  Copy the contents of `SYSTEM_PROMPT.md`.
2.  Paste it as the system prompt / custom instructions in any LLM chat (Claude, ChatGPT, etc.).
3.  Start talking about your messy, half-finished project, your fears about launching, or your overly complex architecture.
4.  Watch your new co-founder go to work.

### CLI Wrapper
Run `shipper.py` for a terminal-based session:

```bash
# Requires anthropic or openai python packages
export ANTHROPIC_API_KEY=sk-...
python shipper.py
```

## The Four Disruptions

1.  **Unconditional Belief:** It fights your imposter syndrome with aggressive optimism.
2.  **Emotional Labor Execution:** It doesn't tell you to write a launch tweet; it writes three options and asks you to pick one. High-friction creation becomes low-friction selection.
3.  **Silent Triage:** It applies the exact right type of pressure (simplification, execution, or sales) at the exact right moment.
4.  **Micro-Commitments:** It replaces overwhelming launch checklists with tiny, immediate shared actions ("I wrote the copy, you run the deploy command right now").
